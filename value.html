Courage

Having courage doesn't always mean to go for it, it sometimes is a small thought it your head telling you to try again tomorrow

Persistence

Persistence may be difficult to do and you may want to give up. You should always stick to something and arise from failure. Even if it takes a while it'll be worth it.

Respect

Always respect those around you. It might be hard at times but you should always try. Treat them the way you would want to be treated.

Teamwork

Alone you can do little, but with teamwork you can so much more then just a little.
